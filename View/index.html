      About

      The purpose of this website is to help students find and sell textbooks. This website was designed and developed in spring of 2015 by a group of USCB Computational Science students. The USCB Textbook Exchange website is a localized web service for the students at the University of South Carolina Beaufort.

      The web service connects students who want to save on textbooks with those who want a shipping free solution. Students have the option to buy, sell, or donate their textbooks. Students can receive payment and textbooks faster than utilizing a retailer while avoiding shipping costs.

      Terms of Service

      The USCB Textbook Exchange serves only as an intermediary service and is not responsible for any sales that go awry. all users are to make exchanges in the library during regular business hours to ensure safety for both parties.
