How do I Register?

You first need to click the Register/Login button. After clicking the Register/Login button you would click the Register button. Once on the Register page, enter the information that is needed for registering. Once it is completed it will send you an email to the email you provided. Check the spam box if you do not see the email and once you find it click the link to confirm. It will then direct you to the home page so you can login from the Login Button.

How do I log in?

You first need to register an account, once you register an account you can then click the Login button and click Login. You will then be prompted to fill out the required fields. After hitting submit it will automatically direct you to the Home Page.

How do I verify my email?

To verify your email you need to go to the designated email address that you assigned your account to. Once you Login to your email address you can check your inbox. Once you find the confirmation email click the link then it will register your account and redirect you to the homepage.

How do I sell a book?

To sell a book all you need to do is to have a registered account and then Login to your account. Once you login to your account you can then click the sell button. After you fill out the listing form to list the book it will then be published to the Listings page for other users to view and buy the book of their choosing.

How do I edit my account?

To edit account you must first be logged in. Once your logged in you can hit the edit profile button. From there you can change any profile settings that you want.

How do I delete my account?

To delete account you must first be logged in. Once your logged in you can hit the edit profile button. From there you can click the delete account button and confirm that you want to be deleted.
