How do I Register?

  1. Navigate to the upper right-hand corner and select Register/Login
  2. Click Register
  3. Register your valid USCB email
  4. Click Register button
  5. Check your email for the confirmation link

How do I log in?

  1. You must have a registered account to login
  2. Navigate to the upper right-hand corner and select Register/Login
  3. Enter your USCB email and password into the respective fields
  4. Click the Log in button

How do I verify my email?

  1. Register your valid USCB email
  2. Check your school email for the confirmation link
  3. Click the link in the email
  4. You may now create your account

How do I sell a book?

  1. Login to your USCB Textbook account
  2. Click the sell tab on the navbar
  3. Fill in the Book details
  4. Click submit to create a new listing

How do I edit my account?

  1. Login
  2. Click the Account Settings Dropdown
  3. Select edit account

How do I delete my account?

  1. Login
  2. Click the account settings Dropdown
  3. Select edit account
  4. Click the delete account button
