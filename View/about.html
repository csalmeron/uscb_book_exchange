About

The purpose of this website is to help students find and sell textbooks. This website was designed and developed in spring of 2015 by a group of USCB Computational Science students

The USCB Textbook Exchange website is a localized web service for the students at the University of South Carolina Beaufort. The web service connects students who want to save on textbooks with those who want a shipping free solution. Students have the option to buy, sell, or donate their textbooks. Students can receive payment and textbooks faster than utilizing a retailer while avoiding shipping costs.

Students that want to sell their textbooks have the option to create listings which include the following: book title, author, ISBN, price, publisher, description, seller's schedule, email, and name
